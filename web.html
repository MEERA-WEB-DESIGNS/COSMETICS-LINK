About Us

Pakistan's Most Trusted
Online Beauty Store

Established in 2023, Cosmetic Link was born from a simple belief: every Pakistani deserves access to authentic beauty products without the risk.

We saw how online shopping for cosmetics in Pakistan was filled with uncertainty -- counterfeit products, no return policies, and the fear of paying for something you haven't seen. That's why we introduced our revolutionary "First Check Your Parcel, Then Pay" policy.

Based in Islamabad, we deliver nationwide with Cash on Delivery, ensuring a 100% risk-free shopping experience. Every order comes with a surprise gift because we believe beauty should always feel special.
